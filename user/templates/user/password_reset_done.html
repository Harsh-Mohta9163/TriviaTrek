Password Reset Instructions

Please follow the steps below to reset your password:

  • Check your email inbox for a password reset link.
  • Click on the link provided in the email.
  • You will be redirected to a page where you can enter a new password.
  • Enter your new password and confirm it.
  • Click on the "Reset Password" button to complete the process.

If you do not receive an email, please check your spam folder or try again.
